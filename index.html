About Me

I get adrenaline from coding and it makes me feel alive. It never really occurred to me just how much I would enjoy completing coding projects until I finished my first project in my beginner python class. Finishing a recreation of space invaders while being able to understand every line of code made me feel amazed. This level of programming inspired me to continue down the path of computer science. In the same way that I was interested in computer science, physics also had a place in my heart. My curiosity in quantum technology and electronics would motivate me to pursue my physics degree. For the past three years, I would grind through physics concepts and see how applicable that they can be for my field. After this year, I soon realized that when my interest in physics was combined with my knowledge in computer science, both would be elevated to a new level.
